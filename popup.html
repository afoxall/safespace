About


Tumblr Savior is a free browser extension created and maintained by Bjorn Stromberg to improve his experience on Tumblr. He thought you might also find it useful and decided to share it with you.


This extension is open source and all source code can be found at https://github.com/bjornstar/Tumblr-Savior.
